Open Data Hub Day 2022

On 20 May, the Open Data Hub Day was held at NOI. A hybrid event about best practices to help understand and make the most of the world of data. This is an increasingly important topic for those working in business and research and was explored in depth with several experts and representatives from leading companies and NOI’s scientific partners.
